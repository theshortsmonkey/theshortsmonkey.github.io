A little bit about me

I am a recently trained full stack JavaScript software developer with bags of real world experience from a career in aeronautical engineering.

Having spent 16 years in the engineering industry designing turbomachinery, an opportunity came up to retrain as a software developer so I jumped at it and enrolled in the Northcoders Software developer bootcamp to prepare me for a new career in the tech industry.

My extensive background in engineering has honed my problem solving skills and given me the ability to understand the whole of the system as well as being able to dive into the critical details. During that time, I also dipped my toe into coding through developing automated analysis processes which ignited a passion and curiosity for learning more about coding which I wanted to pursue.
